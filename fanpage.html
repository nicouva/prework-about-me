Bio!

We Are Scientists is a New York City based band that formed in Berekley, CA in 2000. It consists of primarily lead vocalist and guitarist, Keith Murray, and bassist, Chris Cain, with Keith Carne joining the band on drums for studio recordings and live performances in 2013.

Members List

Keith Murray - Lead Vocalist and Guitarist (2000 - present) Chris Cain - Bassist and Vocals (2000 - present) Keith Carne - Drummer (2013 - present) Michael Tapper - Drummer (2000 - 2007) Andy Burrows - Drummer (2009 - 2014)
